FAQ

Good questions

What should I wear?

We'd love for guests to dress in garden or countryside-smart attire. Think summer florals, linen suits, or smart casual — whatever feels festive and comfortable for an outdoor-then-barn celebration. Please avoid white, cream, or ivory.

Are children welcome?

We love your little ones! Children of immediate family are warmly invited. Due to venue numbers we're unfortunately unable to include all children — if you're unsure, please give us a ring.

Can I bring a plus one?

Your invitation will indicate whether a guest is included. Due to capacity at the venue we're unable to accommodate additional plus ones — thank you for understanding.

Will there be vegetarian / vegan options?

Absolutely — please let us know your dietary requirements on the RSVP form and we'll make sure the caterers have everything sorted for you.

Is the venue accessible?

The barn is fully step-free with accessible parking close to the entrance. Please get in touch if you have any specific requirements and we'll arrange everything in advance.

Can I take photos during the ceremony?

We're having an unplugged ceremony — phones and cameras away, please! This lets everyone be fully present (and means no one blocks our photographer's shots). Phones are very welcome for the rest of the day.

Where can I send a card or gift?

Your presence is really the only present we want. If you'd like to contribute to our honeymoon fund, details will be on the table on the day. Cards can be handed to us personally or posted to: 12 Orchard Lane, Dublin 6.

Who do I contact with questions?

Email us at sarahandtom2025@gmail.com or ring Tom's mum, Patricia, who is our brilliant coordinator: 087 123 4567.
